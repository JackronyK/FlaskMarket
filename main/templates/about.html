About Abuu Market

Abuu Market is a simple, developer-driven online marketplace built with Flask. We aim to provide:

  • Easy browsing of products
  • Secure user accounts and order management
  • Fast checkout with multiple payment options
  • Responsive, mobile-friendly design

Our mission is to empower small vendors and buyers to connect seamlessly online. Whether you are looking for daily essentials or unique finds, Abuu Market has you covered.
